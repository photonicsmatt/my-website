Everything You Need to Know

Photonics Media Virtual Events are free, expert-led online summits covering lasers, optics, imaging, spectroscopy, vision systems, and biophotonics. Published by Photonics Spectra, Vision Spectra, and BioPhotonics magazines, these one- and two-day events feature industry leaders sharing the latest advances and applications.

In 2026, Photonics Media is hosting 16 virtual summits running from February through December. Topics range from Raman spectroscopy and ultrafast lasers to quantum technology, integrated photonics, and multiphoton microscopy.

Yes — all Photonics Media virtual events are free to attend. Simply register ahead of time to access live presentations, Q&A sessions, and on-demand recordings after the event.
